Events

From weddings to company dinners, Alex specializes in providing a rich and warm atmosphere with his solo piano music as the background. Being self-taught, his thoughtful playing style holds an eclectic mix of influences from classic jazz, R&B, church, and pop music. He is currently a regular pianist at the Canlis restaurant in Queen Anne on friday nights. His repertoire includes both jazz standards and renditions of pop songs. He also has experience working with singers and/or other musicians with a rehearsal or two under the belt.

If you are interested in Alex playing for your event, contact him at alexandernamnguyen@gmail.com for more details.

About

A recent graduate from the University of Washington, Alexander Nam Nguyễn is a self-taught pianist, singer, and composer of choral music. He is a Vietnamese-American born and raised in Tacoma, Washington, and currently teaches middle school choir in the Issaquah School District.
